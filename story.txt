The Thirsty Crow

Once there was a crow named Harold. He lived amid the hustle and bustle of the city of Jaipur.

It was the month of May and the Sun was scorching. Harold had flown miles in search of food and was very thirsty because of it.

Harold asked his friend, a monkey called Chunky, for help. Chunky said he knew a house that always had a pot of water for thirsty birds like Harold.
